COOL BIRD I SAW TODAY: THE TIT

Alright, so most people call it the chickadee (from the 'chicka-dee-dee-dee-dee' call) but REALLY it's funnier to call it such. Specifically I saw a "black-capped-chickadee" in the bush over by my birdfeeder, but I couldn't get a good picture due to how fast they flew off!
You can spot one from:

  • Their call (duh)
  • Their black head and white cheeks
  • they're also really common here, like REALLY common. Set out proper suet and you'll see em
