Easy Block Patterns

For places to buy materials, I'm a big fan of Joann fabrics-- they've got a good selection of resources aside and I sew with a Brother CP100x I bought off their online page during a wonderful sale-- a bit past half price too.

Keep an eye out for sales, and they have good jellyroll selections (those little fabric bundles, whether in actual rolls or square packs of folded yardage). Their actual supplies for quilting are pretty decent too, and I got my rotary cutter and mat from the local location too-- along with all my current string.

On the costly-ish side, but not terrible. Great place to look at.

Another option is the Confident Stitch, which has a great (and local!) selection of fabrics. Way more quilting specific utilities too, and I just adore their patterns. Plus, soft fabric.
