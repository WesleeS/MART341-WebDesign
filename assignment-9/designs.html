Easy Block Patterns

For designing a quilt, it all comes down to taste. An easy thing to do is a "sampler quilt", where much like a sampler platter you have a bunch of blocks you sew together in a grid or alternative layout in order to have a huge scale of variety.

There's also medalion quilts, which feature a large central block in the middle.
For starting out, it's worth just thinking of all the ways you can arrange even the simplist tiles. Here's a great demo video of just arranging HST's, and here's a site with a bunch of easy designs to just pick and start.
