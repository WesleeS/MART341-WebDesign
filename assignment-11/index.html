Bird triva, the Allen's Hummingbird is an urban species of hummingbirds! They're from the pacific coast and tragically not from around here. Best chances is viewing them in Cali, and sometimes they go to central america during the winter seasons. Most tend to stay all year though, given California doesn't really go too deep into winter.
